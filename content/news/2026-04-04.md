If you've been looking around here for a while, you'll have figured out that I've got a few different settings that are (supposed to be) internally consistent, but are quite different from one another.  With the latest, my introduction of a name for my "grounded reality, modern day" setting, where you meet the likes of [[Eleanor Whitcombe]] and [[Heidi Price]], [[Frankie Pierce]] and [[Rachel Brooks]], and [Laurence Tremblay](Work%20In%20Progress/Flash%202025-10%20to%202026-09/Characters/Laurence%20Tremblay.md) and [Ashley Gagnon](Work%20In%20Progress/Flash%202025-10%20to%202026-09/Characters/Ashley%20Gagnon.md), I think it might be useful for me to nail down some canon details about these different settings.  So now I'm adding a page dedicated to just that. Originally I'd started out planning to let [[Series]] do that heavy lifting for me, but now that I'm realising I'm going to have my [Spirit Boxing](Spirit%20Boxing) characters appear in my [Friday Flashing](Friday%20Flashing) series alongside the ones from [Warm Front](Warm%20Front), that's just not going to hold up anymore.

So here we go! [Settings](Settings) and [Series](Series) are now officially unrelated.
